Hawaii Honolulu Mission — Week 3

Sunday (July 6, 2025), we got a text, then a phone call from the mission president, confirming that we will not be taking over the far-away wards that the Mundells had visited. Instead, we'll be given a couple of the wards near us in Kaneohe. The call came at 7:58 a.m. (the first meeting  would have started a minute later, at 8 a.m.), and there was no definite assignment, so we continued with our plan to attend the ward our apartment is in, the Kaneohe 4th Ward, at 9:30am. We arrived twenty minutes early, and met some very nice members of the ward.

Monday, we met the sisters at Firestone, where we got their low tire pumped. Then, we attended the district council.

Tuesday, we drove to the temple, and after our session, Sara took this iconic selfie facing the ocean, from the steps of the temple. While there, Bruce felt confirmation that we are in the right place.

Then, Sara and Helaman drove to get Jessica at the airport, while Bruce napped. In the evening we inspected the elders car and apartment.

Wednesday, we prepared for and went into Honolulu. Then signed up for a Costco card with Jessica in Waipio. Drove home and brakes failed near the end. Very scary.

Thursday returned to Honolulu; not a good outcome. To be continued next Monday. Long talk with Mike Perry about the first batch of mail and what to do with each item.

Friday, Bruce walked to Lex Brodies Tire and Brake to inquire about their rates, and met the general manager, Rylan Miguel, a friend of Jasmin's. Satisfied, he walked back to drive the Scion over there, but Sara said that would not be a good idea in case the brakes failed again. He arranged for a tow truck through UCCU elevated checking and rode along, got the car scheduled for diagnosis of brake hydraulics and walked home. Sara and Jessica did some shopping at Target for things needed by an apartment of sisters. Some members came and she dropped off her truck, lending it to us to use to go to church.

Saturday, Bruce requested reimbursement for sister items. Egg and sausage for breakfast. Sara went with Jessica and Helaman to a beach, while Bruce fixed his lunch and napped.
